About Us

NESERA wants to spread our message of hope and compassion. We believe that a single action can make a difference in the community, and that collective action can greatly impact the world. Through advocacy and outreach activities, our team works tirelessly each day to contribute their part to the greater good.

What We Do

1. Diplomat 2016

A national level fest organised by De.paul International College,Mysore


• Members of Nesera served as volunteers for the entire fest.
• One of our volunteer had hosted the events (Master of Ceremony).
• The Treasure Hunt event conducted by us turned out to be very successful.
The institution is looking forward for our help in the events to come.
• We are in the middle works with them to sign a multi-year contract.


2. Karunamayee Foundation

Day care support to specially challenged children


• It is special day care school which looks after mentally challenged and disabled children.
• There are about 45kids in total in this institution.
• The foundation was in need of voluntary support and all the children were looked after by a few staff members.
• Our organisation have been sending volunteers to the institution/foundation on a daily basis to interact with the kids and assist the teachers in their work.
• We also helped the kids to bring out their talents in the form of dance which was held on December 27 th 2016 on the occasion of the Birth Anniversary of Rashtrakavi KUVEMPU ,at the Open air theatre, Manasa Gangotri,mysore.

3. Candle Light March

An incident of molestation on December 31 st night was caught on cctv near Kammanahalli,Bangalore.


• A candle light march organised on the 1 st of January in-front of Mysore palace to raise our voices against this case in particular and in general condemning all sorts of violence against girl children and women.
• We took part in the march along with the kids of Karunamayee foundation and rendered our support demanding to take serious actions against the culprits.
• This event was aired on many news channels.

4. Dance Republic

A charity event organised by Creative Creations at Decathlon,Mysore.


• We participated in the event as volunteers.
• It was dance event where dance trainers taught Zumba to the people assembled there.
• We also made some monetary contributions(from NESARA)towards the cause.

5. Ajithanele

A home for boys who are orphaned


• Volunteers from Nesera on rotation visit Ajithanele to teach children different subjects on daily basis.
• 15 boys will be appearing for the SSLC Public Examination this year.












Our Goals. To bring a change in society

Here at NESERA, we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Our decision making process is informed by comprehensive empirical studies and high quality data evaluation.

We strive to build productive relationships and make a positive impact with all of our pursuits and goals.

We are UG Students coming from different fields striving to bring a change in society. We want our physical power and mental knowledge to be spread all across the needy people.
